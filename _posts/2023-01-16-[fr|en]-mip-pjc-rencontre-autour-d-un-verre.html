Title: meeting over drinks

Description: What is the purpose of these events held on the last Sunday of each month?

Meeting for a Drink is an event at bars and other casual venues that began in August 2022.

Here are the identified goals:

  • Meet with MIP and PJC admins;
  • Meet other volunteers in the association: leaders of collectives and jam organizers;
  • Discover the MIP and PJC atmosphere in off (without the instruments);
  • Please ask your questions about the functioning of the MIP and PJC community but also of AIMP;
  • Start networking: we're still an association with people, if you want to accelerate your integration into the volunteer plans, you need to make yourself known and this is your chance!;
  • Discuss ideas and ambrions of ideas that we could develop on Zikapanam (MIP / PJC or AIMP).

These events take place on the last Sunday of the month from 6-8pm except in July and December.

So don't hesitate any longer to reserve your Sunday to come and meet us!
