Title: recruiting

Description: The MIP community is growing fast! Check out the great projects underway and coming soon.

Collective List

The MIP community created in November 2022 by the Zikapanam association for adult intermediate and beginner amateur musicians, has already seen three collectives develop!

  • Ultra Heavy Riffs Collective (Rock, Grunge, Punk, Hard Rock, Metal) : Leader Jeanne-Charlotte
  • Les Grands Classiques Collective (French Song, Pop) : Leader Maurice
  • Jazz / Blues Collective (Jazz / Blues and some Pop) : Leader Yann

See this link for an updated list of collectives: Updated Collective List on Google Docs

Become a member of a collective

As far as collective members go, if you just want to play music, have fun and share good times in a fun collective, don't hesitate to join the MIP community!

Recruiting bassists and drummers is always the most time consuming so if you are motivated, you will be welcomed with open arms! Please don't hesitate to apply.

It's all happening on Discord! Click on this link to join the Discord: MIP Discord otherwise you can check out the MIP presentation over there Linktr.ee

Becoming a collective leader?

We hope that more collectives will be added to the list (Pop / Rock or Pop / Soul / Funk ...). The qualities of a collective leader: rigor and organization, good communicator and reliable. If you meet all these criteria you are welcome as a leader.
