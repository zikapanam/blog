Title: Types of encounters

Description: Zikapanam has been enriched by different types of encounters since its creation!

DIFFERENT TYPES OF MEETINGS

  1. Les jam-o-studios : in private, associative or commercial rehearsal studios;
  2. The street jams : outside in good weather in places where this activity is tolerated;
  3. Les sorties jams : in bars that have street-side gables and organize jam sessions or open stages;
  4. Remote jams : over the Internet using free software such as jamulus or sonobus (you need a computer, fiber optics and a sound card);
  5. Les Concerts : classical, we prepare a repertoire, an entertainment, attract an audience and play in front of it.
  6. Internet Live : play live and be broadcast directly on the internet!
  7. Concert outings : go and see a free or paying concert together.
  8. Jam evenings : organized by the association, an evening dedicated to jamming and open stages;
  9. Les rencontres informelles : a get-together to chat and remake the world;
